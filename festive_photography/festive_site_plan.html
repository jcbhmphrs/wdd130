Overview

Purpose

My photography business is growing and I need an online presence in order to reach more clients and customers. I want to share my work with the world in an accesible web-format.

Audience

This website will appeal to a wide category of people. Mainly I wish to reach those who are wanting to remember the most important events in life. People who treasure the moments spent with loved ones and want to have photos to remember them by.
