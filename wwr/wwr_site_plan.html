Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to build revenue by increasing awareness of my small business where river guides take people on white water rafting through schedualing and booking river guides.

Audience

My target audience are the adventure seekers that love the convienience of booking thier private adventure in advance. I want to target those who are comfortable using the internet to book thier times. My audience has a need for clear scheduling and easy to operate online resources. This audience will use this website through laptop or desktop access.
